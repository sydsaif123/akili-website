Who We Are

Akili Advisory Group is an Africa-focused independent technology advisory firm. We serve boards and C-suites who face complex technology decisions — digital transformation, platform selection, vendor negotiations, technology governance — without trusted, vendor-neutral guidance.

Too often, boards are left to navigate these choices alone, relying on the very vendors seeking their business. We exist to change that. We bring clarity to complexity, and we stay accountable for outcomes well beyond go-live.
